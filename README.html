AI Prompts Library

This repository curates battle-tested prompts that teams can reuse across development workflows. Every prompt ends with a .prompt.txt suffix so they are easy to search, lint, and embed in prompt catalogs or automation pipelines.

Copy, adapt, ship.

What You'll Find

Prompts are organized by discipline (for example software-developer or maintainer) and then refined into purpose-driven subdirectories like requirements, testing, or authoring. Each document captures the prompt text exactly as you would paste it into your AI assistant, making it trivial to share and version-control improvements.

Suggested Workflow

  1. Browse the catalog and open the prompt that matches your task.
  2. Copy the contents as-is into your preferred AI assistant, swapping placeholders for project specifics.
  3. Contribute refinements by editing the prompt file and documenting the change in this overview.

Roadmap

  • Add prompts for design, product management, and operations disciplines.
  • Create automated lint checks to verify prompt metadata and extension naming.
  • Publish quickstart guides for integrating prompts into IDE snippets or CI templates.

Conventions

Use lowercase directory names, keep prompts as plain text, and document placeholders inside the prompt body.

Feedback

Propose improvements through pull requests or issues describing the scenario you optimized.

Reuse

Feel free to copy prompts into your organization. Attribution is appreciated when sharing externally.
